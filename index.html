Chicken Handi

Take a large pot or wok, big enough to cook all
the chicken, and heat the oil in it. Once the oil
is hot, add sliced onion and fry them until deep
gold.

  • Chicken - 1.2 kg
  • Onion - 5 thinly sliced
  • Tomatoes - 2 finely chopped
  • Garlic - 8 cloves chopped
  • Ginger paste - 1 tbsp

Tandoori chicken

red onions in a large shallow dish. Slash
each chicken thigh three times, then turn
them in the juice and set.....

  • lemons - 2 juice
  • paprika - 4 tsp
  • red onions -2 finely chopped

Chicken Couscous

Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan and
cook the onion for 1-2 mins just until
softened. Add the chicken and fry for 7-10
mins until cooked through....

  • olive oil - 1 tbsp
  • onion - 1 chopped
  • chicken breast - 200g
  • ginger - pinch
  • harissa spice - 2 tblsp
